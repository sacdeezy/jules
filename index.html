🛡️ Safety & Ground Rules

Peace of mind, always included

We know trust is everything. Here's exactly what you can expect — no surprises, no grey areas.

  • 🏡
    Parent Always Present. Juliette is a helper, not a babysitter. A parent or guardian must be home during every session — no exceptions.
  • 🚫
    No Diapering or Bath Time. Juliette focuses on play and companionship — personal care tasks are handled by you.
  • 📲
    Parent-Managed Communication. All scheduling and payments are coordinated through Juliette's parents — not directly with her.
  • 🌟
    A Learning Experience. This project is part of Juliette's journey in developing work ethic, responsibility, and a servant's heart. Your kindness supports that growth.
